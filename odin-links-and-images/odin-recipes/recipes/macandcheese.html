Mac and Cheese

Ingredients
  • Macaroni Pasta
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Flower
  • Milk
  • Paprika
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook the macaroni until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. >Melt the butter in a large saucepan over medium heat.
  3. Peel and finely mince the garlic, then add it to the melted butter and cook until fragrant.
  4. Stir the flour into the butter and garlic, whisking continuously to form a smooth roux.
  5. Slowly pour in the milk while whisking constantly until the sauce becomes smooth and begins to thicken.
  6. Season the sauce with paprika and stir well to combine.
  7. Add the grated cheese a little at a time, stirring until it has completely melted into the sauce.
  8. Fold the cooked macaroni into the cheese sauce and stir until every piece is evenly coated.
  9. Serve the mac and cheese while hot, or transfer it to a baking dish and bake until the top is golden, if desired.
