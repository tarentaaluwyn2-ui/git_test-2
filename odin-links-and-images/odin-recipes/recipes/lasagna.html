Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients

  • meat
  • tomato sauce
  • cheese
  • pasta

Steps

  1. Cook ground meat with diced onions and garlic over medium-high heat until browned, then drain excess fat.
  2. Simmer sauce: Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, Italian seasoning, salt, pepper, and a pinch of sugar.
  3. Develop flavor: Reduce heat to low and simmer uncovered for about 20 to 30 minutes until thickened.
  4. Cook pasta: Boil lasagna noodles according to package directions, drain, and rinse with cold water to stop cooking (skip if using oven-ready noodles).
  5. Mix cheeses: In a medium bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, egg, a handful of Parmesan, chopped fresh parsley, and a pinch of salt.
  6. Base: Spread 1 cup of meat sauce across the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  7. Noodles: Place a single layer of noodles over the sauce, slightly overlapping them.
  8. Cheese: Spread a portion of the ricotta mixture over the noodles, then sprinkle with mozzarella.
