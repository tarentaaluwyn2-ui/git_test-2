Pizza Recipe

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pizza dough
  • Green peppers

Steps

  1. Wash the tomatoes thoroughly and slice them into thin rounds.
  2. Slice the mozzarella cheese into even pieces.
  3. Place the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface and roll it out into your desired shape.
  4. Wash the green peppers, remove the seeds, and slice them into thin strips.
  5. Clean the mushrooms with a damp paper towel and slice them thinly.
  6. Spread the tomato slices evenly over the pizza dough.
  7. Arrange the mozzarella cheese on top of the tomatoes.
  8. Add the sliced green peppers and mushrooms evenly across the pizza.
  9. Add the sliced green peppers and mushrooms evenly across the pizza.
  10. Remove the pizza from the oven, let it cool for a few minutes, then slice and serve.
